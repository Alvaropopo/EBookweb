Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban
J.K Rowling
1999
As Harry Potter enters his third year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, the wizarding world is on high alert after the escape of Sirius Black, a dangerous prisoner from Azkaban. Everyone believes Black is after Harry, since he was a known supporter of Lord Voldemort and allegedly betrayed Harry’s parents, leading to their deaths.
One Piece, Vol. 5: For Whom the Bell Tolls
Eiichiro Oda
1998
Luffy and his crew are locked in battle with the fearsome pirate Captain Buggy, who can split his body into pieces thanks to the power of the Chop-Chop Fruit. As Buggy threatens to destroy Orange Town and reclaim a map to the Grand Line, Luffy steps in to defend the people and his own dream of becoming King of the Pirates.
Harry Potter and the Cursed Child
J.K Rowling
2016
Now a Ministry of Magic employee, Harry Potter struggles with his past and his strained relationship with his middle son, Albus Severus Potter. Albus feels overshadowed by his father's legacy and forms an unexpected friendship with Scorpius Malfoy, the son of Draco Malfoy. The two boys try to right a past wrong by using a Time-Turner to save Cedric Diggory, believing it will undo an injustice. However, their actions create dangerous alternate timelines, including one where Voldemort wins the Battle of Hogwarts.
Naruto, Vol. 7: The Path You Should Tread
Masashi Kishimoto
2001
In this volume, the intense battle between Team 7 and the dangerous rogue ninja Zabuza reaches its dramatic climax. As the mission to protect the bridge builder Tazuna continues, Naruto, Sasuke, and Sakura are pushed to their limits—especially after Haku, Zabuza's loyal companion, reveals the full power of his deadly Crystal Ice Mirrors jutsu.
The Adventures of Sherlock Holmes
Arthur Conan Doyle
1892
This is a classic collection of twelve short stories featuring the brilliant detective Sherlock Holmes and his loyal companion Dr. John Watson. Set in Victorian London, the stories showcase Holmes’s legendary powers of observation and deduction as he solves mysteries that baffle even Scotland Yard.
