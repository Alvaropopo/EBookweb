Atomic Habits
James Clear
2018
Atomic Habits is a practical and insightful guide to building good habits and breaking bad ones through small, incremental changes. James Clear argues that success doesn’t come from huge transformations, but from the compound effect of tiny, consistent improvements—what he calls “atomic habits.” Drawing on psychology, neuroscience, and real-life examples, he introduces a framework known as the Four Laws of Behavior Change: make it obvious, make it attractive, make it easy, and make it satisfying.
Becoming
Michelle Obama
2018
Becoming is a deeply personal memoir by Michelle Obama, the former First Lady of the United States, in which she reflects on the experiences that shaped her identity, values, and worldview. The book is divided into three sections—"Becoming Me," "Becoming Us," and "Becoming More"—tracing her journey from a working-class upbringing on the South Side of Chicago to her time in the White House.
